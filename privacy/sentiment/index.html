Sentiment Privacy Policy

Wednesday, March 4, 2026

This privacy policy explains how Sentiment handles your data when installed from Google Play.

1. Summary

  • Your journal data stays on your device. Entries are created, processed, and stored locally.
  • Encrypted at rest. Data is encrypted using Hive AES encryption. The encryption master key is wrapped and stored using the Android secure storage system.
  • Biometric unlock optional. If enabled, biometrics are used only by the Android system to unlock your local encrypted data.
  • No analytics, no ads, no third-party data usage. The app does not send any data to external servers.
  • Optional Google Play backup. If you enable Google Play backup on your device, Android may back up your encrypted app data to your Google account.

2. Data We Collect (Locally)

Data you create locally

  • Journal entries (text, tags, timestamps).
  • Detected Emotion labels (e.g., “Happy”, “Sad”) generated by the app’s local machine learning model. All content is stored locally and encrypted.

Local device data (stored only on the device)

  • Local identifiers required to manage the encrypted database.
  • Local app preferences (theme, settings, biometric lock state).

None of this data is transmitted anywhere.

3. How We Secure Your Data

  • Local encryption. All journal content is encrypted using Hive AES encryption.
  • Secure key protection. Encryption keys are stored using the Android Keystore system.
  • Biometric authentication. If enabled, biometrics (fingerprint/face) are processed entirely by the device; we do not receive or store biometric information.
  • No servers. We do not operate any servers and do not receive your journal content.

4. Google Play Backup (Optional)

If Android’s Google Play backup is enabled on your device, the OS may back up the app’s encrypted data to your Google account.

Important notes:

  • The app does not control the backup process — it is handled by the Android system.
  • Backups may include encrypted journal data and app settings.
  • To manage backups, visit your Google account or Android Backup settings.
  • Disabling backups will stop Android from backing up your data.

5. Permissions Used

Depending on your app features, the app may request:

  • Biometric authentication — to unlock the encrypted journal locally.

6. Data Sharing

  • No data is shared.
  • No third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising libraries are used.
  • No data leaves your device unless:
    • You choose to export it manually, or
    • It is backed up through the Android Google Play backup system.

7. Data Deletion

  • Delete individual entries from within the app.
  • Clear all app data from system settings (“App info → Storage → Clear data”).
  • Uninstalling the app removes local encrypted data from your device.
  • If using Google Play backup, uninstalling the app does not automatically delete backups — manage them via your Google account.

8. Children’s Privacy

The app may be used by people of any age.
Because data is stored only on the user’s device and never transmitted, we do not collect personal information from any user, including children.
Parents or guardians who manage a child’s device may use device-level tools (screen time, backup settings, etc.) as they see fit.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when features change. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date.
