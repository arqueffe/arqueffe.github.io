WorldWise - Welcome to the WorldWise DevBlog!

7 Mar 2024

This is my first post about WorldWise, I am excited to share my journey!

What is WorldWise?

WorldWise is a mobile application that aims to teach geography using the state of the art technologies of E-Learning. My goal is to give a personalized learning experience to the users, breaking away from traditional learning methods.

Why WorldWise?

I believe that the existing learning applications are not personalized enough. While they can be engaging, they assume that all users learn in the same way. WorldWise is designed to learn from the user for the user. It will adapt to the user’s learning style and pace, making it a truly personalized learning experience.

Features

My ambition is to make WorldWise the best geography learning app out there. Here are some of the features that I’m working on:

  • Knowledge Retention Prediction: WorldWise will predict how well you’ll remember a particular piece of information. This will help you focus on the areas that need more attention.
  • Difficulty Level Adaptation: The app will adapt to your learning pace. WorldWise will identify and adjust the difficulty level to keep you engaged.
  • Exercise Type Adaptation: WorldWise will adapt the type of exercises to your learning style. WorldWise will understand what kind of exercises seem to improve your learning rate and tailor them to your learning style.
  • Progress Tracking: Keep track of your learning journey and see your knowledge grow over time.

Current State

I am currently in the early stages of development but I am making good progress.

Right now, I am working on the core features of the app:

  • Exercise Generation: I am working on generating exercises that are tailored to the user’s predicted knowledge.
  • User Profiling: I am working on the user profiling system that will help the app understand the user’s learning pace.

As of now, you can discover the countries of the world and their capitals given your selected regions and subregions. You can also do practice exercises to test your knowledge on the countries you have discovered. It is possible to see your history of discoveries and exercises, and to see your progress over time.
